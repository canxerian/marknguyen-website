Dreamglass

Art: Gemma Suen
Design: Martyn Gooding, Joe Garbutt
Engineering: Mark Nguyen
Project Management: Joanna Webb, James Glading

Mattress Firm approached the Mixed Reality Studios team and sought to apply Mixed Reality technologies in retail stores and expositions stands.

We used an Azure Kinect, body tracking algorithms and intuitive interaction design to assess customers' postures.
